Best Engineering Colleges in Punjab

The institution is ranked among the best engineering colleges in North India. It is approved by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA)(2009-12 & 2016-18) and the National Assessment & Accreditation Council (NAAC) with Grade ‘A’. Additionally, the institute has earned diverse ranks no longer simplest in Punjab but in North India, forging it one of the best colleges in Punjab. AGC is affiliated with Inder Kumar Gujral Punjab Technical University (IKG PTU), Kapurthala (for engineering and Management courses) whereas the Pharmacy courses are affiliated to IKGPTU and PSBTE & IT. And, for the lately added courses under law i.e. BA LLB and LLB, the institute is affiliated with Guru Nanak Dev University (GNDU), Amritsar.


Amritsar College is possessed with a leading name in providing quality training to its apprentice. It has succeeded in creating a niche for itself with its expert faculty, apt students, and its good infrastructure providing an adequate learning environment to its pupils. Its tremendous growth in a short span of time explicates its success in the education sector. It is an Edcil India Limited (Govt. of India enterprise) partner Institute which connives the wide edges of learning making it a global study center by taking the honor for the acclamation of the learners from various countries like Nepal, Bhutan, Sri Lanka, Burma, East Africa, and other countries, proving to be a Top Engineering Colleges in Punjab.


The college works with the vision of providing the best education with an ideology of Right Place for a Bright Career. AGC is one of the best engineering colleges in Punjab which is considered to be the hub of quality edification. To justify this declaration, AGC is gratified to mention that 3267+ students have been placed in more than 223 agencies till January 2018 through its wonderful placement record. The institution in its venture of creating a brilliant team of future-ready professionals started placements and company visits in 2006 which have continued ever since with its collaboration with the top multinational companies like Cisco, Oracle, Sun Micro System and many more for more improved academics in the near future.
