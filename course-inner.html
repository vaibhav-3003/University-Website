B.Tech CSE College in Punjab

Course Overview

The Course combines the elements of Computer Science and Electrical Engineering making it superlatively cutting edge in the field of engineering. Along with this, having attained a degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Amritsar Group of Colleges, a student gets well versed with technical as well as non-technical skills that are in the demand of the employers today. The Department of Computer Science Engineering at Amritsar Group of Colleges is a premier learning center running software like JCP, OCA, and OCP in collaboration with SUN MICRO-SYS and ORACLE respectively. Many students of AGC having exceptional knowledge have bagged placements at various software and IT industries. This makes AGC as one of the Top CSE Colleges in Punjab.

Duration

Four Academic Years divided into 8 Semesters.

Admission to the Third Semester of Bachelor of Technology Programmes (LEET)

AGC has also a provision of direct admission to the third semester for diploma holders. The discipline in which admission can be allowed will be notified by the I.K.G Punjab Technical University. The candidates who have passed their Three-Years Diploma in any branch of engineering are eligible for admission under this category.

Mode of Admission

Amritsar Group of Colleges possesses its own Scholarship cum Entrance Exam to initiate its admission process. Through this test, the students get an opportunity to earn up to 1.5 Lac off on the total academic fee.
